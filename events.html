WELCOME TO INFINITO

Infinito is the annual sports fest of IIT Patna.

Once a humble initiative, after just three editions, Infinito has already grown into the biggest and most awaited sports fest of Bihar. It is an endeavor to ensure that no talent goes unnoticed. Through Infinito we provide people a platform where they can not only display their skills but also learn and develop the qualities of a true sportsman. Knit together are the virtues of team spirit, undying determination and zeal, and Infinito is an embodiment of them all.

Infinito is a three days fest and these three days see participation from colleges all over India. With a plethora of sporting events and exhilarating cultural nights we leave no stones unturned into ensuring that these three days are filled with fun and thrill and that everyone who is a part of our ever-growing family gets to make the most beautiful of memories.

Let's sweat to glory, together.

WELCOME TO AARAMBH

Aarambh: IIT Patna's annual sports fest for freshers

Aarambh is an exciting and dynamic event that sets the tone for the academic year at IIT Patna. It's a vibrant celebration that blends sportsmanship with the spirit of fun, making it a highly anticipated fest on campus.

Exclusively for freshers, Aarambh offers a unique platform for the newest members of IIT Patna to showcase their athletic talents. It's a great way for them to bond, build friendships, and immerse themselves in the campus culture.

This fest is not just about fun—it's highly competitive, offering freshers a fantastic opportunity to challenge themselves and rise to the occasion. Aarambh is where new talents emerge, making it an event full of promise and potential.

WELCOME TO NSO

NSO: National Sports Organization

The National Sports Organization (NSO) at IIT Patna plays a pivotal role in shaping the overall development of students. Beyond physical fitness, participation in NSO activities instills discipline, teamwork, and leadership skills. These qualities are essential not just in sports but also in academics and future careers, helping students to become well-rounded individuals.

At IIT Patna, the NSO is an integral part of the curriculum, offering a structured sports course that promotes regular physical activity. Students have the opportunity to engage in various sports, honing their skills while balancing their rigorous academic schedules. The sports course is designed to enhance both physical and mental endurance, contributing to a healthier and more focused student body.

By participating in NSO activities, students at IIT Patna gain a break from their academic pressures while also developing essential life skills. The program encourages a culture of fitness and wellness, ensuring that students are well-prepared to meet the challenges of their academic and professional journeys.
