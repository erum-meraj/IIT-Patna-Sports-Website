Inter IIT 2023, Gandhinagar Achievements



Aryan Dhakad won a bronze medal in Long Jump (Male) and secured 4th place in Triple Jump (Male) at Inter IIT Gandhinagar 2023, showcasing his talent in athletics.

Shaurya-2023 Athletics Achievements



At IIT Kharagpur Sports Fest, Ankit Sawate (3rd Year) won bronze in the 1500m race, and Krishna Kant (2nd Year) won bronze in the 5000m race. Preeti Kumari (2nd Year) won two bronze medals in the 400m and 1500m races, demonstrating her remarkable performance at IIT Kharagpur Sports Fest.

Shaurya-2023 Volleyball Achievement



The Women's Volleyball team from IIT Patna won a silver medal at Shaurya-2023, IIT Kharagpur Sports Fest, showcasing outstanding performance and team spirit. The Men's Volleyball team from IIT Patna achieved a bronze medal at Shaurya-2023, IIT Kharagpur Sports Fest, reflecting their strong teamwork and skills.

Parakram 2024 Athletics Achievements



Santosh Yadav (1st Year, UG) won gold in the 400m hurdles and silver in the 400m race at IIT Dhanbad’s sports festival, Parakram 2024.
